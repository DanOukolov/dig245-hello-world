The Filesystem
The filesystem was pretty straight forward and covered some of the same topics that the command line covered. It was basically the command line applied to manipulating files on your computer. It also covered specifics on how files are generally organized and structures within the computer. This included users and permissions, the paths to find the files, and the extensions given to files to tell the computer which apps can open them. Although this module was shorter than the last, I believe it might be even more useful considering how important it is to understand where all your information is kept and how it is organized. I also found the shortuts to be helpful -- although I will need some refreshers every now and then.
